Personal Pronouns

En inglés, hay 7 pronombres personales principales, divididos en 3 grupos:

Pronombres como sujeto (Quien realiza la accion)
English Spain Example
I Yo I eat pizza. (Yo como pizza.)
You Tú/Usted You are kind. (Tú eres amable.)
He Él He runs fast. (Él corre rápido.)
She Ella She sings well. (Ella canta bien.)
It Eso/Ella/Él It is a cat. (Es un gato.)
We Nosotros/as We study English. (Nosotros estudiamos ingles.)
They Ellos/Ellas They play soccer. (Ellos juegan fútbol.)

What is HTML semantics?

HTML semantics is very important to have code that is easy for the browser to interpret.

The semantics of HTML consist of <header>, <nav>, <main> and <footer>.

These tags help us to better structure our code

What is <header> used for?

<header> is used for titles on a website

What is <nav> used for?

<nav> is used for navigation bars on a website

What is <main> used for?

<main> is used to group important information on a website

what is <footer> used for

<footer> is used at the end of a website
